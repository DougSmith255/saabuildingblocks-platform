Team Types at eXp Realty

Understanding Types & Compensation

Domestic Partnership

Team Formation
Two eXp agents that are legally married or have a legally recognized union, with the same named Sponsor and cap reset date.
Minimum Members
2 (1 Primary + 1 Secondary)
Cap Options
Shared $16K combined Company Dollar Cap.
Min. Team Leader Split
None

Full Cap Team

Team Formation
Any agents at eXp Realty. No production requirement.
Minimum Members
2 (1 Team Member + 1 Team Leader)
Cap Options
All members retain full $16K Company Dollar Cap.
Min. Team Leader Split
None

Half Cap Team

Team Formation
Any agents who can document $6M in closed volume or 30 units in the prior 12 months.
Minimum Members
2 (1 Team Member + 1 Team Leader)
Cap Options
Members: $8K or full $16K.
Leaders: $16K.
Min. Team Leader Split
Average of 15% of total Gross Compensation from any reduced-cap Team Member.

Quarter Cap Team

Team Formation
Any agents who can document $40M in closed volume or 140 units in the prior 12 months.
Minimum Members
11+ (10 reduced-cap Members + 1 Team Leader)
Cap Options
Members: $4K, $8K, or $16K.
Leaders: $16K.
Min. Team Leader Split
Average of 25% of total Gross Compensation from any reduced-cap Team Member.

ICON Any agent who pays the full $16,000 Company Dollar Cap is eligible for the ICON Agent Award.
